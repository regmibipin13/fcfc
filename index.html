About Faith Christian Family Church

Faith Christian Family Church is a dynamic teaching Ministry where the word of God is taught in Love, Power, and Simplicity. F.C.F.C. is a wonderful place to learn, to grow, and connect with other people in an energetic and welcoming atmosphere.

Our celebration services are full of powerful worship, solid and practical Bible teaching with a focus on faith, family, church, and finance. We also have an active and exciting children’s ministry. Join us for midweek services, Home Bible study groups, and intercessory sessions that combine prayer and studying the word of God.
