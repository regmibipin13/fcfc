Who We Are

Faith Christian Family Church is a vibrant assembly of believers with a mission to bring glory to God, know Him, and proclaim His Word through evangelism and discipleship.

Our Vision

An assembly of believers impacting and transforming lives with the gospel of Jesus Christ.

Our Mission

To bring Glory to God, know Him, and proclaim His Word through Evangelism and discipleship.

Our Core Values

  • Integrity and Accountability – living in holiness and practicing honesty.
  • Evangelism – outreach for Christ.
  • Discipleship – nurturing believers to be like Christ.
  • Faith and Works – Believing and doing what pertains to faith in Christ Jesus.
  • Community – Value for God-ordained family, partnering, and embracing unity with others.

Statement of Faith

  • We believe in the Trinity: God the Father, the Son, and the Holy Spirit.
  • That Jesus is the Son of God.
  • That the Holy Spirit is a divine person.
  • That all scriptures are the inspired Word of God.
  • That all have sinned and are in need of a Savior.
  • That salvation is provided through Jesus Christ for all men.
  • That healing is available to every believer through Jesus Christ.
  • That the church is an assembly of those who have received Christ as Savior.
  • That there shall be a bodily resurrection of the just and the unjust, followed by judgment.
